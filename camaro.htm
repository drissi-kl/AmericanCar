Chevrolet Camaro

In a segment full of burbling American muscle, the Chevy Camaro ZL1 proves stereotypes are as good as garbage thanks to its great handling, sharp steering, and stonking powertrain. Beneath the hood is a 650-hp supercharged pushrod V-8 borrowed from the last-generation C7 Corvette Z06. Rear-wheel drive is standard, but the ZL1 and zanier ZL1 1LE are each offered with either a six-speed manual or 10-speed automatic transmission.
